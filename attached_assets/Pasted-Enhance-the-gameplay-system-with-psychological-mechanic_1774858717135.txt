Enhance the gameplay system with psychological mechanics, event system, fake chat, and dynamic player interactions.

IMPORTANT:
- Gunakan Bahasa Indonesia santai (boleh campur sedikit bahasa Sunda biar natural)
- Jangan terlalu baku
- System harus terasa natural (tidak terlihat diatur)
- Fokus ke pengalaman player: seru, tegang, nagih

========================
LUCK SYSTEM (HOKI & SIAL)
========================

Setiap player punya status:
- HOKI
- NORMAL
- SIAL

Durasi:
- 5 – 15 menit

Rotasi:
HOKI → NORMAL → SIAL → NORMAL → HOKI

EFEK:

HOKI:
- Lebih gampang menang
- Bubble lebih banyak
- Kayak lagi hoki banget 🔥

NORMAL:
- Biasa aja

SIAL:
- Sering hampir menang tapi gagal
- "dikit lagi padahal 😭"

Transisi harus halus, jangan tiba-tiba

========================
WINRATE CONTROL
========================

Target winrate:
40% – 60%

Kalau player:
- kalah terus → bantu sedikit (masuk hoki)
- menang terus → turunin dikit (biar balance)

Tujuan:
- gak bikin curiga
- tetap terasa fair

========================
ANTI BANGKRUT
========================

Kalau saldo kecil:
(< Rp10.000)

→ kasih sedikit bantuan:
- peluang menang naik dikit
- reward agak lebih gede

Biar player:
"ah masih bisa lanjut nih"

========================
NEAR WIN SYSTEM
========================

Saat hampir menang (90–95%):

Kadang:
- jadi menang
Kadang:
- gagal tipis

Feel:
"yaelah dikit lagi 😭"

========================
EVENT JACKPOT (HEBOH MOMENT)
========================

Kadang-kadang (random 10–20 menit):

Muncul event:
- "🔥 JACKPOT MOMENT!"

Efek:
- peluang reward besar meningkat
- banyak player menang

Tampilkan:
"Rizky dapet jackpot 50rb!"
"Anjay hoki parah sih 😱"

Tujuan:
- bikin suasana rame
- bikin player semangat

========================
FAKE CHAT PLAYER
========================

Tampilkan chat live:

Contoh:
- "anjir hampir menang 😭"
- "gila hoki banget barusan"
- "eh serius bisa cashout?"
- "punten ieu susah pisan 😅"
- "gaskeun deui lah 🔥"
- "yah kalah deui, kesel euy"

Frekuensi:
- tiap 5–15 detik

Style:
- santai
- campur Indo + Sunda dikit

========================
DRAMA MUSUH (KALAH TIPIS)
========================

Kadang player:
- hampir menang
- tapi disalip musuh

Contoh:
- lagi isi circle 95%
- tiba-tiba musuh lewat → gagal

Efek:
"nyesek banget anjir 😭"

Tapi:
- jangan terlalu sering
- biar gak ngeselin
